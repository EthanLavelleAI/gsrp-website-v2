GSRP Server Rules & Conditions

Please read and understand all rules before joining our server. Violation of these rules may result in warnings, kicks, or permanent bans.

Adhere to Terms of Service

01

✓ Follow Roblox & GSRP Terms of Service

Always follow the Roblox and GSRP Terms of Service. It's the foundation of our community. Violating these terms can result in account suspension or permanent ban.

Use Common Sense

02

✓ Common Sense is Crucial

Common sense is crucial. Our moderators have the right to remove you from a roleplay if your actions are unreasonable. When in doubt, ask a staff member for clarification on rules.

No Random Deathmatch (RDM)

03

✓ Intentional Harm Forbidden

Intentionally harming another player without a reasonable in-game cause is strictly forbidden. This keeps the roleplay fair and immersive. You must have valid roleplay initiation before engaging in combat.

No Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

04

✓ Protect Vehicles

Don't intentionally ram, shoot, or push another player's vehicle. It's a quick way to get removed from a scene. Vehicles must be treated with respect during roleplay scenarios.

New Life Rule (NLR)

05

✓ Cannot Return to Scene

If you are killed or removed by a moderator, you cannot return to that scene. This applies even if a panic button is pressed or your mafia requests your presence. You must start fresh and avoid the area.

Do Not Interfere with Roleplays

06

✓ Respect Ongoing Scenes

Stay out of scenes you weren't part of from the start. Respect marked-off areas (like those with cones or tape) to avoid disrupting others. If you see a scene in progress, find another location for your roleplay.

Realistic Avatars Required

07

✓ Human-Like Appearance Only

Your avatar should look like a real person. No wings, tails, antlers, or similar accessories are allowed. For any animal roleplay, you must have permission from a JHR+ staff member before proceeding.

Activity Permissions

08

✓ Staff Approval Required

Hostages, Roadworks, and Checkpoints require permission from an on-duty staff member before you can activate them. This ensures proper roleplay management and prevents abuse.

Safe Zones

09

✓ No Roleplay or Shooting in Safe Zones

Roleplaying or shooting is strictly prohibited in these areas:

  • Both Civilian Spawns
  • Both Fire Department Spawns
  • Sheriff/Police Spawn
  • Department of Transportation Spawn

These areas are protected to allow players to safely join the server and prepare for roleplay.

Vehicle Regulations

10

✓ Exotic & Supercar Rules

All Exotics and Supercars are Booster only. Only players with booster status can drive these high-end vehicles on the server.

Server Rules Acknowledgment

These rules are in place to ensure a fun and fair roleplaying experience for everyone. Please read them carefully to get the most out of GSRP.

By joining GSRP, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to follow all server rules. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse for violation.
